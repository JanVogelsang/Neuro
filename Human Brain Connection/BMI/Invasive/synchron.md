# Synchron

### Stentrode

- Endovascular - Implantation via blood vessels (incision in neck to access jugular vein)
- No electrode decomposition -> 6 months within a blond vessel without vascular occlusion
- Stimulation in deep areas possible -> allows treatment of Parkinson's, OCD, depression, epileptic seizures
- minimally invasive method for deep brain stimulation tested on sheep motor cortext
